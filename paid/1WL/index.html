What we're working on today:

Cardio + ABS

Note:
Warming up the entire body for 2-3 minutes. Warm-up for the first exercise with two sets using lighter weights. Afterward, begin the workout.

1) 3 exercises in one set without a break, the first exercise is 10 jumps, immediately followed by the second exercise, 12 seconds of rope skipping, and then immediately transitioning to the third exercise, 2 squats, 2 upward thrusts, and 2 straight thrusts, for 3 rounds, with a 20-second break between rounds.

Rest 40s

2) 3 exercises in one set without a break, the first exercise is 8 push-ups, immediately followed by the second exercise, 15 seconds of in-place running, and then immediately transitioning to the third exercise, 15 seconds of dumbbell exercises at a faster pace, for 3 rounds, with a 20-second break between rounds.

Rest 40s

3) 3 exercises in one set, the first exercise is a 20-second wall sit, the second exercise is a plank with weight on the back for 40 seconds, and then immediately transitioning to the third exercise, side planks for 15 seconds each side, for 3 rounds, with a 20-second break between rounds.
