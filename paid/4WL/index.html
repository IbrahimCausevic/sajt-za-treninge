What we're working on today:

Chest and Arms

1) Flat Bench Press, 3 sets x 10 repetitions: Position your shoulders optimally, arch your chest, lower the weight slowly to your chest, inhale, lift it up quickly, and exhale.

2) Incline Bench Press, 3 sets x 10 repetitions: Position your shoulders optimally, arch your chest, lower the weight slowly to your chest, inhale, lift it up quickly, and exhale.

3) Here we have 3 exercises in one set without a break. Incline Fly, 10 repetitions, immediately without a break, move to the second exercise for 10 repetitions, and after that to the third one, also 10 repetitions. Repeat this circuit 3 times with a 30-second break between circuits.

4) Cable Crossovers, elbows at shoulder height, one grip, 8 reps, then switch to the other grip for 8 reps. Do 3 sets with a 30-second break between sets.

5) Two exercises in one set, first exercise 8 repetitions, then immediately switch to the second exercise without a break, also 8 repetitions for each arm. Repeat this circuit 3 times with a 30-second break between circuits.

6) Two exercises in one set, first exercise 8 repetitions, immediately switch to the second one without a break, and do 8 repetitions. Repeat this circuit 3 times with a 30-second break between circuits.
