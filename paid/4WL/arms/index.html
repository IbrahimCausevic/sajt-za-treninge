What we're working on today:

Arms

1) Biceps with dumbbells, 10 repetitions per arm, 3 sets.

2) Triceps on the cable straight bar, 3 sets of 10 repetitions.

3) Biceps curls with a curved barbell, 3 sets of 10 repetitions.

4) Triceps with a rope, extend your arms fully, 3 sets of 10 repetitions.

5) Biceps on the cable straight bar, start with the heaviest weight for 8 repetitions, immediately without a break reduce the weight for another 8 repetitions, then without a break reduce the weight again and perform partial reps from 90 degrees upwards quickly for 12 repetitions. Repeat this circuit 3 times with a 30-second rest between circuits.

6) Triceps pushdowns with a twist, 10 repetitions, immediately return to the starting position without a pause and do 10 more repetitions. Repeat this circuit 3 times with a 30-second rest between circuits.

7) Combine 2 exercises for biceps and triceps: Start with 8 slow repetitions for biceps, holding for 2 seconds at the top, immediately without a break, do 8 repetitions for triceps. Repeat this circuit 3 times with a 30-second rest between circuits.
