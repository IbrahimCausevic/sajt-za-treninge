What we're working on today:

Legs + ABS

1) Hyperextension, 3 sets of 10 repetitions, hold the weight against your abdomen.

2) Squats, feet slightly wider than shoulder-width apart, keep your back straight while descending, 3 sets of 10 repetitions.

3) Leg press, place feet shoulder-width apart, do not fully extend legs to avoid knee injury, 3 sets of 10 repetitions.

4) Dumbbell lunges, 3 sets of 10 repetitions.

5) Leg extension for the front thighs, 3 sets of 10 repetitions.

6) Leg extension for the rear thighs, 3 sets of 10 repetitions, push back strongly until the end.

7) Stair climbing, 10 minutes.
