What we're working on today:

Shoulders + Back

1) Smith machine in front of the head, lower the bar to chin height, 3 sets of 10 repetitions.

2) Smith machine behind the head, 3 sets of 10 repetitions.

3) Here we have 2 exercises in 1 set, the first exercise consists of 8 repetitions, immediately without a break, move on to the second exercise for 8 repetitions. Complete 3 rounds with a 30-second break between rounds.

4) Middle shoulders on the machine, 3 sets of 10 repetitions, lifting to a height of 90 degrees.

5) Rear shoulders, 3 sets of 10 repetitions.

6) Front shoulders with dumbbells, 3 sets of 10 repetitions, raising the dumbbells to shoulder height.

7) Front shoulders, lifting dumbbells at a slight angle above shoulder height, 3 sets of 10 repetitions.
